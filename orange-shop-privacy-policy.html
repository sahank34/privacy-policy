OrangeShop Privacy Policy

Collection and Use of Information

OrangeShop does not collect any personal information such as name or email address from its users.

Search Functionality

When you use the search functionality on OrangeShop, we display search items along with their prices. We do not track or store any user data related to these searches.

Shopping Cart

When you add items to your shopping cart on OrangeShop, we do not collect or store any personal information about you.

Total Calculation

When you check the total of items in your shopping cart, we perform the calculation based on the prices of the items you added. We do not store any personal information for this purpose.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us.
